Our Policies

Clear guidelines for a seamless experience at Machine Yard, for both renters and owners.

General Policies

At Machine Yard, we aim to create a fair and transparent platform for renting and providing heavy machinery. These policies apply to all users—whether you're renting a machine or listing one for rent. Please read carefully to understand your rights and responsibilities.

Rental Terms

  • For Renters: Machines are rented on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis, as specified by the owner. Minimum rental periods may apply.
  • For Owners: You must clearly state availability, rental duration, and any additional terms (e.g., fuel included or excluded) when listing your machine.
  • Rental agreements are finalized upon booking confirmation and payment.
  • Machines must be returned in the same condition as received, barring normal wear and tear.

Payment Policies

  • For Renters: Full payment is required upfront via our secure payment gateway. A refundable security deposit may also be charged, depending on the owner's terms.
  • For Owners: Payments are disbursed to your account within 3-5 business days after the rental period ends, minus a 10% platform fee.
  • All transactions are in [Your Currency, e.g., USD]. Prices include taxes unless otherwise stated.
  • Late returns may incur additional fees, calculated at 1.5x the daily rate.

Cancellation & Refunds

  • For Renters: Cancellations made 48 hours before the rental start date are fully refundable. Within 48 hours, a 50% fee applies. No refunds for no-shows.
  • For Owners: You may cancel a booking up to 72 hours before the start date without penalty. Late cancellations may affect your account standing.
  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.

Responsibilities & Liability

  • For Renters: You are responsible for operating the machine safely and as intended. Any damage beyond normal wear must be reported and may be deducted from your security deposit.
  • For Owners: You must ensure your machine is in good working condition, fully serviced, and meets safety standards before each rental.
  • Machine Yard is not liable for accidents, injuries, or damages arising from misuse or negligence.
  • Insurance is recommended for both parties—renters may opt for our coverage plan at checkout.

Questions?

If anything’s unclear, feel free to reach out to us at support@machineyard.com or call us at +1-800-MACH-YARD.
